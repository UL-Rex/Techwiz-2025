About Us

Welcome to Global Vision College. A Hub of Learning, Innovation, and Community Established in 1956, Global Vision College, located in Lahore, Pakistan, is committed to shaping bright futures through quality education, modern facilities, and a vibrant campus environment. Affiliated with the Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan since 1980, we have grown into a center of excellence, offering diverse programs in Science, Arts, Business, and Technology. Recognized as a top tier institution by HEC in 2023, our state of the art labs and inclusive learning environment empower students to excel.

Our Signature Events

Global Vision College hosts a diverse range of annual events designed to foster creativity, collaboration, and community engagement. The year begins with Sports Days and Tournaments in March, where inter-college competitions in cricket, football, and athletics promote fitness and teamwork. In April and November, students participate in Community Outreach and Social Impact Programs, including blood donation drives and charity initiatives to serve society. Academic Conferences and Seminars in October provide a platform for exploring fields like AI, robotics, and innovation through expert-led discussions. The college also organizes Workshops, Career Fairs, and Open Days in September, offering students valuable opportunities to connect with industry leaders and explore career paths. Cultural Festivals and Annual Galas in December bring vibrant celebrations of music, dance, and drama, showcasing student talent and creativity. Finally, in June, Graduation Ceremonies and Award Nights are held to honor academic excellence and extracurricular achievements, marking a memorable conclusion to the academic journey.

Our Mission

Global Vision College, since its HEC affiliation in 1980, is committed to providing a transformative learning environment that blends academic rigor, practical skills, and moral values. The college strives to offer high-quality, inclusive, and innovative education across diverse fields, while encouraging research, creativity, and entrepreneurship to address both national and global challenges. It is dedicated to developing leaders who embody integrity, social justice, and a spirit of community service, and to building strong collaborations with industry, government, and international institutions to enhance learning and create a meaningful impact.

Our Vision

Global Vision College aspires to be a global leader in higher education, renowned for advancing knowledge, promoting innovation, and serving humanity. We aim to nurture graduates who are academically competent, socially responsible, and globally competitive, contributing to the progress of Pakistan and beyond.

Organizing Bodies

The Governing Body

Responsible for overseeing the strategic growth of the institution, shaping policies, and ensuring that all initiatives align with the long-term vision and mission of Global Vision College.

Academic Council

Plays a vital role in maintaining academic excellence by designing, monitoring, and improving curricula, promoting innovative teaching methods, and encouraging research and scholarly activities.

Student Societies and Clubs

Organize events like Hackathons, Cultural Fests, and community projects, giving students opportunities to showcase talents, build leadership skills, and promote teamwork.

Industry & Alumni Relations Committee

Connects with industry and alumni to provide mentorship, internships, career opportunities, and collaborative projects for student growth.
